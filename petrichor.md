# Petrichor

**Pronunciation:** /ˈpɛtrɪkɔːr/

**Definition:** The pleasant, earthy smell produced when rain falls on dry soil.

**Etymology:** From Greek *petra* (stone) + *ichor* (the ethereal fluid that is the blood of the gods).

**Example Usage:** The first drops of summer rain brought the welcome scent of petrichor, a promise of renewal to the parched earth.
